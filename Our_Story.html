How OHOO Candles Started

Ohoo candles was created in the year 2023, started selling scented hand made candles at first and eventually grew, and expanded the products offered. The name idea Ohoo came from Allister, he loves listening to music and when he heard of the album of an artist named Jung Wheein, he was inspired by the name of the song. The song features a lovely, relaxing, and soothing vibes.

Inspired by the song of the artist, he referenced the song and created the shop's name ohoo. As the song's vibes are lovely, relaxing, and soothing, he applied these vibes to the products the shop offers.

Ohoo candles is dedicated and compassionate about their products, offering customers the best satisfation they can experience. With various products you can choose from, Ohoo candles is committed to offer everyone quality products.
